The data collected through the app will be encrypted on the phone.

We will separate your account information (name, email, contact information, etc.) from the study data (the responses to phone surveys and the measurements from the phone itself when performing activities).

We will use a random code instead of your name on all study data. This code cannot be used to directly re-identify you. Information about the code will be kept in a separate secure system. Only key personnel from the study team may connect your study data to your name and account information if needed.

We will NOT access your personal contacts, other applications, personal photos, texts, emails, or websites visited.
